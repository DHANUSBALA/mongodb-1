Find all the information about each products

Find the product price which are between 400 to 800
    ans:db.collection.find({product_price:{$gte:400,$lt:800}})

Find the product price which are not between 400 to 600
    ans:db.collection.find({product_price:{$not:{$gte:400,$lt:600}}})


List the four product which are grater than 500 in price 
    ans:db.collection.find({product_price:{$gt:500}}).limit(4)

Find the product name and product material of each products
    ans:db.collection.find({},{"product_name":1,"product_material":1,_id:0})
